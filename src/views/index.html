Supremo

The best variety, for the processing of which the latest technologies are used, large and even grains. It has a rather rich taste, velvety aroma. Medium-bodied coffee with a subtle fruity aftertaste. A very harmonious, slightly fruity and long-lasting coffee, the equivalent of good champagne in the coffee world! Country: Colombia

Extra

Slightly inferior in terms of quality, but, nevertheless, also has a high class. The size of coffee beans is slightly smaller than that of a supremo, and this indicator is one of the determining factors when sorting coffee beans. The taste of coffee is very strong and full. Country: Colombia

Excelso

Excelso coffee is softer and more acidic than supremo. It is obtained by mixing the other two - superior and extra. The drink turns out to be softer and with higher acidity, with a pronounced aroma and a hint of prunes. Excelso is produced by mixing two of the highest quality varieties, supremo and extra. Country: Columbia

Antigua volcanic

The taste of the most famous variety "Antigua volcanic" is distinguished by a complex, heavy taste with bitterness, strength and exquisite aroma with hints of smoke. Country: Guatemala. This type of coffee is native to mountainous areas.

Santos

It is considered the best and most demanded. World standard made from real arabica. It combines mild astringency, light sourness and chocolate aftertaste. The taste is due to the fact that in Brazil there are practically no hills that give coffee beans sourness and bitterness. Country: Brazil
